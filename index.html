Our Process

How We Make Our Ghee

Every jar of Milkoji ghee is crafted with patience, precision, and dedication to traditional methods.

01

Fresh Milk Collection

We source pure A2 milk from healthy desi cows raised on local farms. Each morning, fresh milk is collected and immediately transported in hygienic conditions.

02

Curd Preparation

The milk is boiled and cooled to the perfect temperature, then cultured with natural starter to create thick, creamy dahi. This step takes 8-10 hours of careful monitoring.

03

Traditional Bilona Churning

Using the ancient bilona method, the curd is hand-churned in a wooden vessel to separate white butter from buttermilk. This preserves all nutrients.

04

Slow Heating Process

The butter is slowly heated in a heavy-bottomed vessel. This transforms butter into golden, aromatic ghee while removing moisture and milk solids.

05

Hygienic Packaging

Once cooled, the ghee is carefully filtered and poured into sterilized glass jars with airtight seals, locking in freshness and nutritional value.
